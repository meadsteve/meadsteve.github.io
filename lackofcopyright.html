Lack of copyright

Everything on this blog I fully release in to the public domain. This means you don't need to ask my permission to re-publish, change, borrow, modify etc. etc. You don't even need to attribute it to me or link it back to me (though I would appreciate it).

Why?

Knowledge wants to be free. All human discoveries are built on top of what came before and I want to add no barrier stopping this. By fully sharing the products of my mind hopefully others can go on and build better things. My inspiration (and a better written, more lucid, explanation) for this decision can be found here Zen Habbits - Uncopyright.
